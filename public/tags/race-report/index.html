The Rut 2014 - Race Report

September 4, 2015

1-Year Anniversary This weekend (09/05/2015) is the 2015 Rut in Big Sky, Montana. That means it’s been one year since I had the opportunity to race there. I never got around to writing a race report and decided this would be the best time to do so. Overview The Rut is becoming a very popular race as it is one of the few true sky/mountain races in the USA. It is featured in the SkyRunner World Series, along with the USA Series.
